HOW TO PLAY

  1. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
  2. Player 1 is O and Player 2 is X. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
  3. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (vertical, horizontal, or diagonally) is the winner.
  4. When all 9 squares are full and there is no winner, it's a draw.
